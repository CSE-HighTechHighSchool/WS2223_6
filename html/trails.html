Allaire Orange Trail

A generally smooth trail with over 200 feet of elevation gain. The trail is blazed orange, although some blazes may appear to be red. It is entirely in the woods, and has many tiny trails, so keep on trail. The ground isn't too rough and should be a pleasent trail.

Stokes Select Trail

A very challenging trail that is very rocky and should only be attempted by experienced bikers. There are amazing views at the top and the trail is quiet and well maintained. Come prepared for a strenuous and long ride up.

Manasquan Reservoir Loop

An easy trail that has many bikers and hikers. Beautiful scenery around the reservoir and very flat and well kept. Very good beginner route that will give you a nice introduction to mountain biking. The trail can get crowded, but is large so the nature can still be enjoyed.
